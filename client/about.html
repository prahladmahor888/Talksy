About Talksy

Connecting the world, one random video call at a time.

Founded with a mission to break down barriers and foster global connections, Talksy is a premier platform for random video chatting. We believe that the best conversations often happen with people you've never met before.

Whether you're looking to practice a new language, find a friend in a different country, or just cure boredom, Talksy provides a safe, fast, and fun environment to do so.

Microphone & Camera Access

To use Talksy, you must grant permission to access your webcam and microphone. This allows you to see and hear your chat partner. We do not record your calls.
