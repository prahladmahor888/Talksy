Safety Center

Your safety is our top priority. Read our guidelines to stay safe.

Community Guidelines

We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, bullying, nudity, or illegal content. Users found violating these rules will be banned immediately.

Protect Your Identity

Never share personal information like your full name, address, phone number, or financial details with strangers online.

Report Abuse

If you encounter inappropriate behavior, please use the report button immediately. Our team reviews reports promptly.

Warning

Talksy is designed for users 18+. Interactions are random, and while we strive for safety, user discretion is advised. If you are a minor, please leave this site immediately.
